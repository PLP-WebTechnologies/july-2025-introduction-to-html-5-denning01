Accessibility Best Practices

Why Accessibility Matters

Accessibility ensures that people with disabilities can fully interact with and understand your website. This includes providing proper landmarks, alt text, and clear navigation.

Example Accessibility Features

  • Use of <header>, <main>, and <footer> for landmarks
  • Meaningful link text
  • ARIA labels for improved screen reader navigation

SEO Basics

Importance of Semantic HTML for SEO

Semantic tags help search engines understand the meaning and structure of your content, improving your page's visibility in search results.

Basic On-Page SEO Tips

  1. Use descriptive and unique titles
  2. Include relevant keywords naturally in headings and content
  3. Provide a clear meta description
  4. Maintain a logical heading structure
